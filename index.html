Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I trust this is truely organic

    This is produced by group of trained farmers with the technical support and careful farmlevel monitoring from our agronomists and it is tested and certified from PGS India

  • What is the primary difference between organic and inorganic vegetables ?

    Organic vegetables are grown without synthetic pesticides, herbicides, and chemical fertilizers, while inorganic (conventional) vegetables may be grown using these synthetic inputs.

  • Are organic vegetables more expensive than conventional ones ?

    Yes, organic vegetables are typically more expensive than conventional ones. Organic farming methods often require more labor and can be less efficient, leading to higher costs.

  • Are organic vegetables tastier ?

    The taste of vegetables can be subjective. Some people believe that organic vegetables have a more natural and robust flavor, while others may not notice a significant difference.

  • Do organic vegetables have a longer shelf life?

    Organic vegetables may have a shorter shelf life because they lack the synthetic preservatives used in conventional farming. Proper storage is essential to extend the shelf life of both organic and conventional produce.

  • Why to eat seasonal fruits and vegetables ?

    Our organic fruits and vegetables follow the seasonal cycle and are only available when they grow naturally. In our consumer-driven society, people expect all fruits and vegetables all year round which forces farmers to cultivate fruit and vegetables outside of their season. They are more likely to require chemicals to aid their growth in that situation.
